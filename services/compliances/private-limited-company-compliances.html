Private Limited Company Annual Compliances

Home / Compliances & Returns / Private Limited Company Annual Compliances

Annual compliances are mandatory for all Private Limited Companies to maintain active status and avoid penalties. We provide complete compliance management services.

Annual Compliance Requirements

  • Board Meetings (minimum 4 per year)
  • Annual General Meeting (AGM)
  • Annual Return filing (Form MGT-7/7A)
  • Financial Statements filing (Form AOC-4)
  • Income Tax Return filing
  • Director KYC (Form DIR-3 KYC)
  • Commencement of Business (if applicable)

Board Meetings

  • Minimum 4 board meetings required per year
  • Maximum gap of 120 days between meetings
  • Minutes must be recorded and signed
  • Notice to be sent to all directors

Annual General Meeting (AGM)

  • Must be held within 6 months of financial year end
  • First AGM within 9 months of first financial year end
  • Approval of financial statements
  • Appointment/reappointment of directors and auditors

ROC Filings

  • Annual Return (Form MGT-7/7A) - within 60 days of AGM
  • Financial Statements (Form AOC-4) - within 30 days of AGM
  • Both forms must be certified by professionals

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Late filing fees start from ₹100 per day
  • Company may be struck off
  • Directors may be disqualified
  • Criminal prosecution in serious cases
