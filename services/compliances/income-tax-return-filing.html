Income Tax Return Filing

Home / Compliances & Returns / Income Tax Return Filing

We provide comprehensive income tax return filing services for individuals, businesses, and companies. Ensure compliance and maximize tax savings with expert guidance.

ITR Forms for Different Taxpayers

  • ITR-1 (Sahaj): Salaried individuals
  • ITR-2: Capital gains, multiple house property
  • ITR-3: Business/Professional income (individuals/HUF)
  • ITR-4 (Sugam): Presumptive taxation
  • ITR-5: LLP, AOP, BOI
  • ITR-6: Companies (other than claiming exemption u/s 11)
  • ITR-7: Trusts, political parties, institutions

Due Dates

  • Individuals (non-audit): 31st July
  • Audit cases: 31st October
  • Companies: 31st October
  • Late filing allowed till 31st December with penalty
